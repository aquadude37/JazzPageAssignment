Jazz Music: Freedom of Expression

Jazz music is a genre of music that focuses a lot on improvisation, freedom of expression, and complex harmonies. It was popularized from the New Orleans Area, and has been built upon and expanded by many famous musicians and visionmaries throughout the years. The instruments used in this music are focused around a lot of brass and woodwind instruments, with a strong emphasis on the piano and drums. Some other instruments used are the saxophone, trumpet, and trombone. It has been a major influence on many other generes of music, especially Hip Hop and R&B. Many of the most well-known jazz musicians are still listened to today, and are still kmown for their contributions to the genre and music in general.


Some of the most famous jazz musicians include:

  • Louis Armstrong
  • Duke Ellington
  • John Coltrane
  • Miles Davis
  • Art Blakey
  • Ella Fitzgerald
  • Thelonious Monk
  • Charles Mingus
